THE MARKET

Nigeria consumes 5 million MT of Rice annually but only produce 2.6 million MT thus filling the gap with import.

However the closing of Nigerian borders to foreign rice, the quality gap between local and foreign has been further exposed.

In comes Simi Celebrations Rice, we are bringing in ‘foreign’ quality local rice to give Nigerians a true choice for the first time.

With 90% of our first 50 MT batch pre-ordered, we plan to build a long lasting system that not only puts food on your table but also money in your pocket.
